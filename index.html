Article of The Week

New ₹5 Lakh Health Insurance for Seniors 70+ launched under Ayushman Bharat, providing coverage for all elderly Indians. Learn Eligibility, Benefits, and More. The Government of India has Expanded its flagship Healthcare Program, “Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY)”, to include Health Insurance Coverage for all Senior Citizens aged 70 years and above. This Scheme offers Health Insurance worth ₹5 lakh per year for eligible seniors, regardless of their socioeconomic status or income. With this initiative, India takes a significant step towards ensuring accessible healthcare for the elderly, reducing the financial burden on families, and improving public health across the nation.
